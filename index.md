## 🛠 Troubleshooting Common Issues

If you encounter problems, look at these solutions before you seek extra help.

*   **No sound:** Check your Audio Setup settings. Ensure your sound card shows in the menu.
*   **High latency:** Adjust your buffer size in the audio settings. Lower values reduce delay but require more CPU.
*   **Crashing:** Disable background programs that use intense memory. Check your Windows update status.
*   **MIDI controller not responding:** Verify the USB connection. Go to Controller Manager to ensure the device is active.
*   **Missing library files:** Click "Relocate" on your folder list. Point the software to the new path of your music files.
